# Frequently Asked Questions

**What is business automation?**
Business automation involves using software to execute recurring tasks or processes without manual effort.

**How can automation help small businesses?**
It accelerates lead response times, eliminates manual data entry, standardizes customer onboarding, directly contributing to improved profit margins.

**Is AI expensive for small businesses?**
No, cloud-based microservices and API integrations allow small businesses to deploy AI for a fraction of the cost of hiring.
